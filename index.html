About

Diana helps individuals of all ages negotiate personal and professional development. She recognizes the needs of students and young adults, and supports parents as they care for themselves and their loved ones. Diana has extensive experience in sport and can assist individuals and teams deal with the realities, pressures, and transitions incumbent with competition. Her practice is informed by evidence-based practices including emotion focused, cognitive behaviourial, and family system therapies.

Areas of practice include:
  • Disordered Eating, Body Image and Self Esteem
  • Anxiety and Depression
  • Relationships

Services

  • Individual Psychotherapy
  • Group Counseling
  • Speaking and Educational Presentations
  • Nutritional Counseling & Meal Support with associate Registered Dietitians

Diana's clinical work is supervised by Dr. Barbara Virley, Registered Psychologist.
